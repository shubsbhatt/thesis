We are

On a personal note, as students of the interdisciplinary master program of MediaArchitecture at the Bauhaus University Weimar, when asked what Media Architecture is, we always have difficulty answering due to its ambiguous nature. We, therefore, want through this thesis to provide an answer not only to others but to ourselves as well. Furthermore, we want to pursue an approach that will combine two different perspectives, that of an architect and that of an interaction designer.
